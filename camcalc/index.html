Aircraft Orientation at Capture
These values rotate world-space offsets into the aircraft's body frame.

From the DCS info bar: use the true (second) value from HDG, not magnetic. Enter PTH, AOA, and BNK as shown.

Stationary, level captures produce the most accurate results due to rounding and unsurfaced values such as yaw.
